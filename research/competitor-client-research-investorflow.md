InvestorFlow is the leading industry cloud for alternative assets (PE, VC, real estate, infrastructure, private credit). It provides CRM, investor relations, deal management, and digital engagement tools — built on Salesforce. Based in Menlo Park, CA.

**Scale**: 150+ clients, 25 of top 50 alternative asset managers, $6T+ in assets, 750 funds, 90,000 LPs.

## Strategies to Find All InvestorFlow Clients

### 1. Technology Footprint Detection

- **BuiltWith / Wappalyzer**: Scan websites for InvestorFlow JavaScript, iframes, or investor portal subdomains (e.g., `investors.firmname.com` powered by InvestorFlow).
- **Salesforce AppExchange reviews**: InvestorFlow is listed on the Salesforce AppExchange. Check reviewer profiles — reviewers often work at client firms.
- **DNS/CNAME records**: InvestorFlow-powered investor portals may CNAME to InvestorFlow infrastructure. Tools like SecurityTrails or DNSDumpster can reveal these.

### 2. LinkedIn Intelligence

- **InvestorFlow employee connections**: Browse connections of InvestorFlow sales/CS staff (Chris Ashrafi — Chief Client Officer, Todd Glasson, Brian Marchand). Their 1st-degree connections at other firms are likely clients.
- **LinkedIn "InvestorFlow" keyword search**: Search posts/articles mentioning InvestorFlow. Filter by industry (Financial Services, Investment Management).
- **Job postings**: Search job boards for "InvestorFlow" as a required skill. Firms hiring for InvestorFlow experience are clients. Try: `"InvestorFlow" site:linkedin.com/jobs` or `"InvestorFlow" site:indeed.com`.
- **LinkedIn Sales Navigator**: Filter by companies that follow InvestorFlow or whose employees engage with InvestorFlow content.

### 3. Event & Conference Attendee Lists

- **InvestorFlow Client Councils**: They hosted councils in NYC (40+ firms attended) and London (Surveyors House, Westminster). Look for social media posts, photos, and event recaps mentioning attendee firms.
- **Industry conferences**: ILPA Summit, SuperReturn, PEI events — check if InvestorFlow presented alongside named clients.
- **Webinar registrations**: InvestorFlow hosts webinars; co-presenting firms are likely clients.

### 4. Public Filings & Regulatory Data

- **SEC ADV filings**: Investment advisers sometimes disclose technology vendors in Form ADV Part 2A (brochure). Search SEC EDGAR for "InvestorFlow".
- **RFP databases**: Public pension funds (CalPERS, CalSTRS, NYC Comptroller, etc.) sometimes disclose CRM/IR vendor selections in public board minutes or RFP awards.
- **FOI/FOIA requests**: For public pension fund clients, file FOIA requests asking about investor portal or CRM vendors.

### 5. Third-Party Databases

- **Apps Run The World**: Maintains a paid [InvestorFlow CRM customer database](https://www.appsruntheworld.com/customers-database/products/view/investorflow-crm) broken down by industry, geography, and company size.
- **G2 / TrustRadius**: Check reviewer profiles on [G2 reviews](https://www.g2.com/products/investorflow/reviews) — reviewers sometimes identify their company.
- **Pitchbook / Crunchbase**: May list InvestorFlow integration partners or technology stack data for PE/VC firms.

### 6. Web Scraping & OSINT

- **Google dorks**:
  - `"InvestorFlow" "investor portal"` — find firms referencing their InvestorFlow-powered portal
  - `"powered by InvestorFlow"` — footer attribution on investor portals
  - `site:investorflow.com "client" OR "partner" OR "case study"`
  - `"InvestorFlow" filetype:pdf` — presentations, case studies, or whitepapers naming clients
- **Wayback Machine**: Check archived versions of investorflow.com for client logos or testimonials that may have been removed.
- **Press releases**: Search PR Newswire / BusinessWire for `"InvestorFlow"` — client announcements often appear here.

- **PERE, Infrastructure Investor, Private Debt Investor**: Sister publications covering InvestorFlow's target markets.
- **Search**: `"InvestorFlow" site:peievents.com OR site:privateequityinternational.com`
