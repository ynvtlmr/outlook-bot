# Funded

Electronic subscription document solution for private market fund launches.

**Tagline:** An intuitive, easy-to-use way for investors to subscribe to private market funds.

## What It Does

Funded enables sponsors, attorneys, and investors to manage the entire subscription process digitally. Originally built by Kirkland & Ellis, now owned by Gen II. It replaces paper-based subscription workflows with smart digital forms, e-signatures, and collaborative tools.

## Track Record

- $350B+ capital raised
- 25,000+ subscriptions processed
- 500+ funds supported
- 2,500+ completed closings

## Key Features

- **Smart Digital Subscription Forms** -- Intuitive questionnaire that fills in the subscription document, integrated with e-signature. Eliminates paper and wet signatures.
- **Investor Passport** -- In-platform investor profile remembers LP answers. 90% of Funded investors reuse their data for subsequent funds.
- **Real-Time Closing Dashboard** -- Sponsors track all prospective investor subscriptions and readiness to close.
- **Collaboration Tools** -- Multiple team members and stakeholders work within one system.
- **Roll-Forward** -- Previously used subscription forms and templates are retained for the next fundraise, saving significant legal fees.
- **Data Digitization** -- Subscription data integrates with downstream systems and processes.
- **24/7 Support**

## Who Benefits

- **Sponsors (GPs):** Real-time tracking, fully electronic process, quick setup
- **Investors (LPs):** Streamlined subscription via intelligent questionnaire, profile reuse across funds
- **Attorneys:** Built by lawyers for lawyers. Setup in as little as one day (competitors take weeks). 50% savings in time reviewing subbooks.
