# Sensr Portal

AI-powered investor portal for the private capital industry.

**Tagline:** First-of-its-kind AI-powered investor portal for private markets (launched February 2026).

## What It Does

Sensr Portal streamlines the entire fund lifecycle -- fundraising, capital calls, data reporting, and investor communications -- in a single branded, white-labeled portal. Fund managers manage funds, investors, and contacts in one place. The AI layer transforms passive document retrieval into active, conversational data engagement.

## AI Capabilities

- **Intelligent Document Search & Summarization** -- Ask natural language questions across capital call notices, distribution notices, quarterly/annual reports. Get summarized answers, key figures, and relevant excerpts instantly.
- **Real-Time Data Querying** -- Pose complex analytical questions about performance, capital balances, and portfolio metrics in plain language. Receive aggregated visualizations and explanations.
- **Personalized Insight Generation** -- Identifies trends, anomalies, and critical updates relevant to a specific investor's portfolio.
- **General Ledger Connection** -- Connected directly to Gen II's general ledger for validated financial responses. Many portals with disconnected data layers cannot offer this.
- **Ring-Fenced AI Security** -- AI accesses only data specific to the investor and the platform. No exposure to the broader internet.

## Core Features

- **Document Management** -- Store, sort, search, and deliver agreements, PCAP, K-1s, and reports with encryption and MFA.
- **Granular Permissions & Audit Log** -- Per-contact permissions on every document and email. Full audit trail of who accessed what.
- **GL Data Feed** -- General ledger data flows directly into the portal, no spreadsheets needed.
- **LP Cash Flow Access** -- Investors see underlying data for approved financials, capital calls, and distributions.
- **Real-Time Document Posting** -- Publish documents within seconds of approval with built-in review workflow.
- **Integrated Digital Subscriptions** -- Seamless transition from data room to Funded subscription to onboarding.
- **Fundraising Tools** -- Landing pages for lead gen, email campaigns, website integration.
