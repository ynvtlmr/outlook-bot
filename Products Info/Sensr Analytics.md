# Sensr Analytics

Performance reporting and fund data analytics platform for private equity and alternative investment firms.

**Tagline:** "Access and analyze your fund data in a way that's meaningful to you."

## What It Does

Sensr Analytics gives investment teams clear insights into fund- and investor-level returns. It provides aggregation and drill-down analysis across funds, investments, and investors -- with tools for attribution, benchmarking (including PME), and scenario analysis.

## Key Features

- **Connected** -- Integrates directly with Gen II's platform. Also supports "bring your own data" for firms consolidating from multiple administrators.
- **Agile** -- Push-button responses to ad hoc LP requests for CFOs, IR teams, and senior management.
- **Intelligent** -- Dynamically calculates investor and portfolio metrics (IRR, TVPI, DPI, RVPI) -- gross, net, levered, and unlevered -- driven by industry standards. Customizable by investment and investor attributes.
- **Managed** -- Turn-key solution with direct data integration, automated validations, and Gen II Client Service team review.
- **Intuitive** -- User-friendly interface to aggregate, report, and analyze across fund families, vehicles, investments, and investors.
- **Accessible** -- Secure, cloud-based. 24/7/365 on desktop and mobile.

## Microsoft Office Plug-Ins

### Sensr Excel
- Access Sensr data directly in Excel with custom templates.
- Automate data retrieval for dynamically updated spreadsheets.
- Advanced analytics for visualization, trend analysis, and predictive modeling.

### Sensr PowerPoint
- Embed Sensr charts and graphs directly in slides.
- Auto-sync values, charts, and graphs for reusable presentations on-demand.

## Investor Access Module

Investors get direct access to their own accounts for a nominal fee per investor:
- Fund-by-fund performance (IRR, TVPI, DPI, RVPI)
- Benchmarking against public and private indices
- Capital account summary (commitment, contributions, distributions, balance)
- Cash flow transparency with interactive visualizations
- Ring-fenced data: investors cannot see other investors' data; unfinalized data restricted until "LP Ready"
- Multi-factor SSO; no additional order forms needed to add investors
