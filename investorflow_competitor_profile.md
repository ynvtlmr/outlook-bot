## Products & Capabilities

### Core Suite
- **CRM** — Investor relationship management, built on Salesforce
- **Investor Portal** — Branded self-service LP portal (document access, capital accounts, statements)
- **Fundraising & Distribution** — Capital formation, pipeline management, fundraising communications
- **Deal Sourcing & Pipeline** — AI-driven capital deployment for PE, credit, RE/infra
- **Investor Services** — Automated workflows, secure document delivery, reporting
- **Fund Reporting** — Investor and fund-level reporting

### AI Capabilities (2024+)
- AI-powered investor insights
- AI-driven deal sourcing and capital deployment
- Entity recognition and data enrichment
- "Defragmentation" of investor information (per Kleiner Perkins CIO)

### Key Features
- **Smartlists** — Replicate Excel functionality in Salesforce
- **Interaction Tracking** — Auto-captures meeting notes and essential info
- **Email Plug-ins** — Correspondence + post-meeting notes without leaving platform
- **Outlook Add-in** — Email integration (confirmed via subdomain: outlook.addin.apps.investorflow.com)
- **Data Feeds** — Integration with Yardi, MRI Software accounting systems
- **Configurable Portals** — Branded, personalized investor experiences

### Products by Asset Class
- **Private Equity & Venture Capital** — Front office management, fundraising, deal flow
- **Real Estate & Infrastructure** — Deal collaboration, asset management (enhanced via Coyote)
- **Hedge Funds** — Capital raising, performance reporting
- **Private Credit** — Deal sourcing, investor services
- **Funds of Funds** — Multi-manager reporting

## Corporate History & Acquisitions

### Cloud Theory Merger + Series A (2022)
- Merged with **Cloud Theory**, a CRM incubated by **Blackstone**
- FundEngine was "designed and built in partnership with the Blackstone Innovations team"
- Raised **$30M Series A** from **Ambina Partners**
- Combined InvestorFlow's investor portal with Cloud Theory's Salesforce-native CRM

### Coyote Software Acquisition
- Acquired **Coyote Software**, UK-based SaaS leader for commercial real estate
- Brought **50+ CRE clients** in UK and Europe
- Coyote served **3 of the top 5 real estate investors** (combined AUM £200B+)
- Originally connected to **M7 Real Estate** / **Oxford Properties**
- Named clients: Nuveen, LGIM, Royal London Asset Management

## Competitive Positioning

### Stated Differentiators
- **Only provider** integrating fundraising, deal sourcing, pipeline management, reporting, AND investor services in one cloud-native suite
- **Salesforce-native** — not a bolt-on; deep CRM integration
- **AI-first** approach (2024+) for capital formation and deployment
- **Full lifecycle** — from fundraising to investor servicing

### Target Market
- Alternative asset managers (PE, VC, RE, credit, infrastructure, hedge funds, FoF)
- Fund administrators

- Wealth managers
